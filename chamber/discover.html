Smithfield City

History

Smithfield was founded in 1859. It was named after John Glover Smith, who was a prominent local leader and early settler. Smithfield was primarily an agriculture town. With Summit Creek providing easy access to water the land was ideal for farming and raising livestock.

Demographics

The first population count of Smithfield was in 1890. There were about 1,500 people. The current population of Smithfield is about 15,172. Since it was founded Smithfield has never had a population decrease.

Activities

Smithfield offers a variety of fun activities for residents and visitors. Outdoor enthusiasts can explore Smithfield Canyon, which has many beautiful campsites and hiking trails. For younger children, the Smithfield splash pad provides a refreshing escape during the hot summer months. For older children and even adults there is a skate park. Additionally, the city boasts several parks where people can play, relax, and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.
