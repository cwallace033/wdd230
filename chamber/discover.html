Smithfield City

Demographics

The first population count of Smithfield was in 1890. There were about 1,500 people. The current population of Smithfield is about 15,172

History

Smithfield was founded in 1859. It was named after John Glover Smith. Since it was founded Smithfield has never had a population decrease.

Activities

There are many fun things to do in Smithfield, Utah. There is a beautiful canyon full of campsites and hiking trails. Smithfield has many parks to play at as well. It also has a splash pad for the hot summer months.
