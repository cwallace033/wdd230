Membership Levels and Benefits

Np Membership (free)

  • Access to networking events
  • Basic listing in the online directory
  • Monthly newsletter subscription
  • Participation in community service projects

Bronze Membership ($100/year)

  • All benefits of NP Membership
  • Enhanced directory listing with logo
  • Discounts on event fees
  • Access to members-only workshops

Silver Membership ($250/year)

  • All benefits of Bronze Membership
  • Priority placement in the online directory
  • Two free event tickets per year
  • Business spotlight in the monthly newsletter

Gold Membership ($500/year)

  • All benefits of Silver Membership
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities
  • Invitations to VIP events
  • Dedicated support from Chamber staff
