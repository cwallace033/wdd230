Navigation

Coming from Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry Dock

  • Exit the Dock Area: Head towards the main exit of the dock.
  • Turn Right onto Av. Rafael E. Melgar: Walk along the waterfront for about 800 meters.
  • Turn Left onto Calle 3 Sur: Walk straight for 300 meters.
  • Turn Right onto Av. 5a: Scoots Rentals will be on your left after 200 meters.

Coming from Terminal Puerta Maya

  • Exit the Dock Area: Head towards the main exit of the terminal.
  • Turn Left onto Av. Rafael E. Melgar: Walk south along the waterfront for about 1.5 km (1 mile).
  • Look for Scoots Rentals: The location will be on your left, easily visible with a sign and scooters parked outside.
