Site Plan Name

The name for the site will be Smithfield Commerce. This is because it will be centered around the town of Smithfield.

Site Purpose

This website will function as a place for people in Smithfield to see what events will be taking place.

It will also be an ideal place to see what business are located in Smithfield and find their contact information.

Scenarios

What events are happening this month in Smithfield?

What service opportunities are upcoming?

How to contact the Chamber of Commerce?
